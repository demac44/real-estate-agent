Tina McCarty


Standing By YOU Every Step of The Way

With 30 years experience in the Real Estate Industry , you can feel confident that all your real estate needs will be looked after , and I will be there Standing By YOU Every Step Of The Way! Starting my career in 1988 , as a receptionist , working my way through as a deal secretary , administrator and then an Executive Office Administrator for a large downtown Royal Lepage Brokerage in Toronto , allowed me to spend time with my two children growing up , participating in their schooling , and their organized sport events.

In 2007 , with the support and encouragement of Royal LePage Real Estate Services Inc. , it was time to broaden my knowledge , and take the real estate courses with Ontario Real Estate Association ( OREA ) to obtain my Real Estate license in Ontario.

In 2009 , I joined Prudential Ronan Realty , Brokerage in my home town of Tottenham . In 2013 Prudential Ronan decided to afflate with Coldwell Banker . Hence , we are under the umbrella of Coldwell Banker , independently owned and operated .

A FULL Time REALTOR® with the upmost integrity , it would be my pleasure to represent you with all your real estate needs. Whether you are buying, selling or have a friend/family member looking to buy or sell , please call me.

Perhaps you are looking to renovate, or need a tradesperson in a specific field, give me a call, I would me more than happy to refer you to a trusted resource.

Proudly Standing By You Every Step Of The Way, and because of all my past clients, it gives me great pleasure and honor to share with you my accomplishments of being an award winner since my first year in real estate, allowing me to be a top producing REALTOR®.
